Who I Am

Driven by curiosity,
defined by action

I'm Asim Ali Abbas, a Grade 12 Computer Science student at Aga Khan Higher Secondary School, Gilgit. I'm applying to Habib University to pursue a degree in Computer Science — because I believe technology, applied thoughtfully, can solve real problems for real communities.

From writing C++ programs that solve mathematical equations to building school management systems, from leading my school's ICT Club to tutoring juniors in Mathematics for 57 hours, I look for every opportunity to learn by doing and give back by teaching.

I completed Google's AI Essentials Specialization on my own time — five courses, fully self-directed. I attended a climate leadership bootcamp funded by the Canada Fund for Local Initiatives. I earned an Honourable Mention at MUN. I won football championships. Every experience has made me sharper, more resilient, and more committed to using education as a force for change.
